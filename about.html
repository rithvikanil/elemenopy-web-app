SO, WHO ARE WE?

Simply put, we are a space for discovery and learning; created by experienced educators who believe children need a nurturing yet engaging environment to grow in.



And with growth, comes change. Children are constantly transitioning as they develop, and at Elemenopy, they find themselves in a happy, creative and secure space which prepares them to make these transitions smoothly.

MARIYAM ABDUL AZEEZ

- started her life as a mom in Jeddah where her husband was settled.

- The lack of good quality education there pushed her to join her son's schools as a teacher. And pursued her Bachelors in education while being a full-time mom and teacher.

- Then as her kids grew she progressed to a principal.

- and finally, when she felt there was more to offer to the field of education she set up a school of her own. This was the biggest challenge of it all -in a country where women have very little say in things.

- First venture. 25 years ago Little kingdom Preschool.

- She currently runs Al Maqased International School and Arwa international School both British curriculum schools in Jeddah.

- Her dream to start to bring back her knowledge started off with co-founding Spring Feld House of children in her town of origin - Thallassery. A purely Montessori school.

And back to her hometown Chennai.
